## Introduction

PrebuiltUI offers a large collection of ready‑to‑use **UI Components** and **Templates** built using `Tailwind CSS`. Everything is fully customizable, copy‑paste friendly and free to use.

Use this documentation to explore components, templates and guides for integrating PrebuiltUI into your projects.

---

### Features

Some of the features of PrebuiltUI are:

-   **Easy to Use**: All components are written using pure Tailwind CSS.
-   **Framework Agnostic**: Works with Next.js, React, Vue or plain HTML.
-   **Fully Customizable**: Every component is fully customizable, copy‑paste friendly and free to use.
-   **Community Driven**: Stay connected with the PrebuiltUI community.

> PrebuiltUI is free for commercial and personal use.
